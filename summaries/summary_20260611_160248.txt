## Meeting Summary

The Q3 Product Roadmap Review meeting focused on finalizing three key features for Q3: notification redesign, CSV export functionality, and mobile push notifications. Decisions were made regarding the implementation details for each, including badge count truncation for notifications, asynchronous processing for large CSV exports, and the approval of mobile push notifications for a September launch. Additionally, a critical API rate limit issue affecting dashboard performance was discussed, and a short-term caching solution was approved while a long-term budget approval for an enterprise plan upgrade is pursued.
